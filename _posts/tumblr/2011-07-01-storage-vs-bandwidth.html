An Interesting info-graphic relating storage vs. bandwidth. It seems to be sponsored by BackBlaze an online secure storage company so I’m not sure what they have to gain by showing that it is cheaper to buy more off-line storage in the form of hard-drives.

That said, with the roll out of the NBN in Australia perhaps we’ll start seeing the bandwidth vs. storage cost curve start to head in the opposite direction.
